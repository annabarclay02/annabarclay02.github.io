A Teacher's Guide to Young People & Media Literacy

Overview

Media literacy is a key skill to navigating the online world effectively and accurately.

Rampant misinformation and fake news, as well as the overstimulation of social media has weakened young peoples' attention spans and media literacy skills, causing serious problems with their consumption of online media and content.

This website aims to prepare and equip teachers and educators of young people with the skills to inform their students on the importances of consuming media critically and forming healthy online habits. It will be used to inform them on the importance of media literacy skills and use real life examples to relate the information to their experiences online.
