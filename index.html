Young People, Pop Culture & Media Literacy

Overview

Media literacy is a key skill to navigating the online world effectively and accurately.

Rampant misinformation and fake news, as well as the overstimulation of social media has weakened young peoples' attention spans and media literacy skills, causing serious problems with their consumption of online media and content.

This website aims to prepare and equip young people with the skills to consume media critically and form healthy online habits. It will inform them on the importance of media literacy skills and use real life examples to relate the information to their experiences online.
